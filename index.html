Frequently asked questions.

Can I see SimpoPay in action before purchasing it?

Yes you can, kindly click on this link or follow us on all social media pages to see the app in action in real life scenarios also how othe features work.

What are the requirements for using SimpoPay?

You must be at least 18 years old and reside in Kenya (for now) or its territories to use SimpoPay. This is because its the legal age to have an ID and open financial accounts in your name .

Can I use SimpoPay on different devices?

  • Yes you can, SimpoPay is linked to your unique username and password.

  • Once installed, you can login using your username and password, the application will however require your fingerprint to authorize any transaction.

Do you have a free trial?

SimpoPay is free to use, we do not charge to sign up or do any transaction. You only get charged standard transaction fees if using your card or MPESAaccount directly in the app.

How does SimpoPay handle my privacy?

All data is encrypted and no one has access to your information including us.

We are using state of the art security for our applications and have been licensed by the Central Bank Of Kenya.

I have an issue with my account

  • You can use the in-app chat feature to get assistance 24/7.

  • Our customer care numbers are active 24/7 to assist with any issue with your account.
